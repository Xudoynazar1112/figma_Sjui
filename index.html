About.

Bajui superpack India limited.

We are a leading masterbatch manufacturer having begun our operations three decades back in Nagpur, Maharashtra India. We are one of the leaders in masterbatches for specific segments of Industrial packaging and with our four plants, our current production capacity is 120 KT/annum. We are an ISO 9001: 2015 certified company from TUV Nord and it is our endeavor to produce products meeting stringent quality standards consistently with an aim of achieving near-zero defects and rejects.
